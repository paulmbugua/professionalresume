FunzaSasa Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 24, 2025

FunzaSasa (“we”, “us” or “our”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application FunzaSasa (the “App”).

1. Information We Collect

  1. 1.1 Personal Information You Provide

    • Account Information: name, email address, profile picture (if you sign-in with Google or create an account).
    • Communications: messages between tutors and students, feedback or support requests.
    • Payment Information: when you purchase recorded classes or tutoring sessions, we collect billing details via our third-party payment processor (we do not store full credit card numbers).

  2. 1.2 Automatically Collected Information

    • Device & Usage Data: device model, OS version, unique identifiers, crash reports, and how you interact with the App.
    • Analytics: in-App events (e.g. which screens you visit), provided by services such as Firebase Analytics or other analytics tools.
    • Location (optional): if you grant permission, we may collect approximate location to tailor certain location-based features (e.g. finding local tutors).

  3. 1.3 Third-Party Data

    • Google Sign-In: your basic Google profile (name, email, profile picture).
    • Firebase (optional): if you use certain features (e.g. chat, notifications), we may store related usage data in Firebase.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide & Improve the Service: authenticate you, manage your tutoring sessions, deliver content, and personalize your experience.
  • Communication: send you confirmations, updates, support responses, and marketing (only if you opt in).
  • Analytics & Research: monitor and analyze trends, usage, and performance to improve the App.
  • Security & Fraud Prevention: detect, investigate, and prevent activities that may be illegal or violate our policies.
  • Legal Compliance: comply with applicable laws and respond to lawful requests.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share it with:

  • Service Providers: Google (for Sign-In), Firebase, our payment processor, Supabase (backend), bundletool, EAS Build, and other vendors who help run or analyze the App.
  • Tutors & Students: to the extent necessary to facilitate session bookings, file exchanges (e.g. PDFs, videos), or ClassVault purchases.
  • Legal Authorities: if required by law or to protect rights, property, or safety.
  • Business Transfers: in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, where personal data may be among transferred assets.

4. Data Retention

We retain your information as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. Usage data and logs may be kept for up to 24 months for analytics and fraud-prevention purposes, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

5. Your Rights & Choices

  • Access & Correction: you may view or update most of your account details in the App settings.
  • Deletion: you can request account deletion; we will erase your personal data within 30 days, except any data we are legally required to keep.
  • Opt-Out: you may opt out of marketing communications at any time by following the unsubscribe link or contacting us.

6. Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organizational measures (e.g. SSL/TLS, encrypted storage) to protect your data. However, no Internet transmission is completely secure—please use strong, unique passwords.

7. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed or stored in servers located outside your country (e.g. in the U.S. or EU). By using the App, you consent to such transfers under applicable privacy laws.

8. Children’s Privacy

FunzaSasa is intended for users 13 years and older. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently done so, please contact us to have that data deleted.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. If we make material changes, we’ll notify you via the App or by email before the update takes effect.
